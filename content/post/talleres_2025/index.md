## Bioinformatics Workshops in 2025

Here are the workshops held in 2025 with links to their sections on the RSG Argentina workshops:

1. **[Estadística Básica y Aplicada con herramientas bioinformáticas](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/estadistica-basica-y-aplicada)** – Learn basic and applied statistics with R/RStudio and Python.  
2. **[Análisis multivariado con Python](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/analisis_multiv_python/)** – Conceptual and practical introduction to multivariate analysis for biological data.  
3. **[Prácticas de programación efectiva](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/practicas_programacion_efectiva/)** – Best practices for clean and efficient code in Python and R.  
4. **[Explorando los servidores Galaxy: herramientas de bioinfo al alcance de todos](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/explorando_servidores_galaxy/)** – Learn to use Galaxy free bioinformatics web platform.  
5. **[Habilidades de Presentación Efectivas](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/habilidades_presentacion_efectiva/)** – Build clear, persuasive scientific presentations.  
6. **[Utilización de SQL en BD biológicas](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/sql_bases_biologicas/)** – Introduction to SQL for biological databases.  
7. **[ABC de la bioinformática](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/abc_bioinformatica/)** – Introductory workshop for beginners in bioinformatics.  
8. **[Genómica bacteriana: Escherichia coli como paradigma](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/genoma_bacteriana/)** – Bacterial genome analysis with Galaxy.  
9. **[Introducción práctica a Nextflow](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/nextflow)** – Intro to Nextflow for reproducible workflows.  
10. **[Análisis y ensamblado de genomas eucariotas en Galaxy](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/genoma_eucariota/)** – Quality control and assembly of eukaryotic genomes.  
11. **[Análisis de amplicones 16S: del secuenciador al perfil de microbioma](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/metabarcoding/)** – Process 16S sequencing data for microbiome profiling.  
12. **[Bioinformática y Drug discovery: De la estructura 3D al fármaco](https://rsg-argentina.netlify.app/workshops/drug_discovery/)** – Basics of drug discovery from 3D structure to docking.
